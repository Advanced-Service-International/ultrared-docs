Frequently Asked Questions

How do I check my card balance?

Open the Simera app and your available balance is displayed on the home screen. You can also tap the eye icon to show or hide it.

How do I recharge my card?

Tap the "Recharge" tab at the bottom of the app, select your card, enter the amount, and confirm the transaction.

What should I do if I forget my PIN?

Go to Cards → PIN and use the "Forgot PIN" option. You'll receive a confirmation email once it's been reset.

How do I block my card?

Navigate to the Cards tab, select your card, and tap "Block Card". Contact support immediately if you suspect unauthorized use.

Why was my transaction rejected?

Transactions may be rejected due to insufficient funds, security restrictions, or card status. Check your transaction history for details or contact our support team.
